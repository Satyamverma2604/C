🖥️ C Programming Output MCQs (Ternary & Logical Operators)

1. Ternary chain for three numbers:
int a = 10, b = 20, c = 15;
printf("%d", a > b ? (a > c ? a : c) : (b > c ? b : c));
a) 10
b) 15
c) 20
d) 25

b) Mid
c) High
d) Error
